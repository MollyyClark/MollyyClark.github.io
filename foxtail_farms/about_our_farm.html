Where we got the name

Why choose us?

Freshness always Guaranteed or 100% money back. All of our produce is grown right here on our farm and all of our dairy comes from our very own foxtail farm cows.

Who takes care of the farm?

The farm is maintained 365 days a year by my husband weston and I as well as our very small but powerful team of 4 other employees.

Fox Tail Farm, LLC was established by turning an empty 5-acre lot into a farm in 2014 and became certified organic in 2016. We grow a variety of vegetables, both in the open field and in high tunnels. The high tunnels allow us to grow year-round and keep fresh vegetables through all seasons.
